Hi! I'm Thomas. I recently completed the Quantitative Fellowship Program with the Minneapolis Fed and currently work as a quant at the NY Fed.

I'm interested in studying the macroeconomic effects of changing inequality as it relates to finance, history, politics, and technology.

Prior to graduate school at the City University of New York (CUNY), I worked as an economist for the American Society of Composers, Authors and Publishers (ASCAP) for five years, while moonlighting as a concert photographer in New York City. I almost always prefer to be on snow or in water. And as a proud Czech from Madison, WI, I adore all things Wisconsin and Česky.
